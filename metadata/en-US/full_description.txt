ZKyNet VPN is a free, open-source VPN client that prioritizes privacy and ease of use.

Key Features:
• WireGuard and AmneziaWG protocol support for enhanced security
• Import and use your own custom VPN configurations
• Auto-tunneling based on network conditions
• One-tap connect/disconnect
• Kill switch protection
• No logging policy
• 100% open source (MIT License)

Perfect for users who want control over their VPN configurations while maintaining privacy. Compatible with standard WireGuard configs and specialized AmneziaWG setups for bypassing deep packet inspection.

Based on WG Tunnel by Zane Schepke, enhanced with additional privacy features.
